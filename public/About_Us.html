About us

A student space for better photography.

The club brings together students who want to practice, teach, experiment, and document life around campus and Ruston.

Our focus

We work across different styles and mediums of photography. Some meetings are practical photoshoots, some are discussion-based, and some are built around sharing what members have made.

Members come from different majors and experience levels, which keeps critiques useful and shoots approachable.

Community

We use photography to notice the campus, Ruston, and the people around us with more care. The gallery exists to keep that work visible.
